I work on large-screen interfaces and digital-physical experiences, with a background in product design. My work includes a broadcast management system for a cultural memorial and a venue dashboard for the Shanghai Astronomy Museum.

I focus on clarity and structure, aiming to make systems easy to navigate and intuitive to use, with visual design supporting how they are understood and used.

主要从事大屏界面与数字与实体结合的体验设计，具备产品设计背景。参与过文化纪念场馆播控系统以及上海天文馆场馆管理看板的设计。

关注信息结构与系统逻辑，致力于提升系统的可理解性与可用性，使视觉表达更好地服务于信息传达与实际使用。
